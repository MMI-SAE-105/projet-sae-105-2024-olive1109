About

My name is Noëlie, I am 18 years old and I am a student at the MMI department of Montbéliard.

We had to create a website for our individual project of the first semester, I hope you liked it!

For this project, we had to choose an everyday object and introduce it either in the past or in the present. So I chose to create the site of a cinema news journal in 2500, called Cinario. Inside, you will find an article about a camera from today found in their time, and many other news about cinema.

More about me, I am passionate about video and cinema, I love watching movies, especially those that have just been released. But I also love making my own videos and cinematic films.

In the future, I would potentially like to start my own visual communication company, to help professionals or individuals with visual communication, such as posters or promotional videos.
