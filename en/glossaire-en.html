Glossary

Cinéphile:

Movie enthusiast and connoisseur.

Original Soundtrack:

Magnetic tape containing the music intended to accompany a film.

Remake:

A new version or a reworking of an audiovisual work.

Pioneer:

Person who paves the way for something, who is the first to do something: The pioneers of computing.

Blockbuster:

A blockbuster is a big-budget, high-spectacle film that attracts a large audience.

Adaptation:

Transformation of a literary, theatrical, musical, cinematic, etc., work to make it accessible to an audience different from the one for which it was originally designed.

Speculation:

Theory, abstract research.
