A Day, A Film Music: The Original Soundtrack of Ready Player One

In this year 2500, let's dive into the fascinating history of the original soundtrack of "Ready Player One".

A Day, A Film Music: "Neural Symphony": The Original Soundtrack Composed in the Mind of the Viewer

In 2500, composer Aria-X9 revolutionized the concept of film music with her creation for "Quantum Odyssey". Her "Neural Symphony" is the first original soundtrack composed in real-time in the mind of each viewer.

A Day, A Film Music: "Galactic Echo": The First Original Soundtrack Composed by a Collective Artificial Intelligence

The year 2500 marked a turning point in the history of film music with "Galactic Echo", the original soundtrack of the space blockbuster "Stellar Nexus". This unique work is the first composition entirely created by a Collective Artificial Intelligence (CAI) named Harmonia.
